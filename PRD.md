1. Product Overview
Summary

This product provides private, secure Large Language Model (LLM) infrastructure deployed locally within an organization's internal network or private cloud. It enables organizations in highly regulated industries to leverage AI capabilities while maintaining complete control over sensitive data.

The system allows organizations to build and deploy AI agents, automation workflows, document analysis tools, and internal assistants without exposing proprietary, financial, medical, or legal data to public AI services.

In addition to the LLM infrastructure, the platform optionally includes cybersecurity audits and WISP (Written Information Security Program) certification, helping organizations meet regulatory compliance requirements.

Organizations in industries such as:

handle highly sensitive data protected by regulatory frameworks, including:

FTC Safeguards Rule / WISP

Public AI tools such as cloud-based LLM services present significant risk because they may:

This creates legal exposure including:

Regulatory fines

Compliance violations

Loss of licensure

Civil liability

Criminal penalties in extreme cases

Many firms therefore cannot safely adopt AI, even though it could significantly increase productivity.
